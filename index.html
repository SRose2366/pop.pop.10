How we found a need of viewers

A need for viewers, in other words, is a reason or reasons why they view the content they like. To find these reasons, we performed research and collected the reasons that were the most important and common.

Definition of Streaming

From the Merriam-Webster dictionary, streaming is defined as ‘the act, the process, or an instance of streaming data or of accessing data that is being streamed’.
In other words, streaming is viewing streaming content, or uploading streaming content.

Types of Streaming Content

The types of streaming contents are TV shows, movies, podcasts, music videos. These content types are what almost all people know, watch and listen to. Other types of content are Documentaries, Sport Shows and more that we can also watch on the tv, or on our devices.

Platforms used for streaming

The platforms often used for streaming are YouTube, Twitch, Spotify, and Facebook.

Viewers analysis for each streaming category

Podcast:

Out of 31 responses we had 22 that favored podcasts over Q&A and Educational Content. Out of 22 we had 16 Females and 6 Males, The ages ranged from 14 - 16 yrs old.

Q and A:

Out of 31 responses we had 3 that favored Q&A over Podcast and Educational Content. From the 3 all were Female, The ages were all 15yrs old.

Educational Content:

Out of 31 responses we had 6 that favored Educational Content over Podcast and Q and A. Out of 6 we had 4 Females and 2 Males, The ages ranged from 15 - 16 yrs old.
